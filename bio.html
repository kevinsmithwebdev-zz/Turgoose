Rachel Turgoose Austin Area Singer, Saxophonist, and Teacher

Rachel Turgoose is an up-and-coming jazz vocalist and instrumentalist in the Austin area. Although new to the rich, live music culture of Austin, Texas, she is no stranger to the spotlight. Rachel has performed across the globe as both a vocalist and woodwind instrumentalist, taking her from her hometown in Pennsylvania to stages located on the west coast, South Pacific, Australia, and beyond. As a cruise line entertainer, Rachel performed a wide variety of music ranging from jazz, rock, classical, Latin, Motown, and hip hop (just to name a few!).

The Austin and San Antonio areas are stocked with incredible musical talent, and Rachel has taken full advantage! Rachel has performed as the featured vocalist in these areas as well as establishing and maintaining her own musical groups and projects.

In addition to performing, Rachel maintains a large private studio of woodwind students where she teaches clarinet, flute, and saxophone. Earning a Bachelors degree in Music Education, along with multiple teaching certifications, has allowed Rachel to gain an extensive background as an educator. Her passion for music continues to inspire and encourage students to shine through their playing and performances.
